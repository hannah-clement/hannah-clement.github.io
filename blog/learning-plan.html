A description of your own strengths and limitations in relation to learning

My strengths of learning are that I am a very hard worker, I am willing and able to put the time into my tasks and will spend more than the allocated time to further my learning and understanding. This is a double edged sword where my time management does become a limitation of mine. I need to be able to implement one of the time management strategies that we explored in the prep foundations. One that did particularly stand out to me was the pomodoro technique where I use a specified period of time and work within that to achieve the task. Moreover, I will need to be okay with trusting the 'just in time' learning strategy which I think will massively aid in my time keeping skills holding off on the time sucking granular level that I can go to. Another limitation I have is that I am not a great speller - I will have to be veeeeery aware of that when writing my code.

I will also be setting a firm BUT achieving a routine which allows me to achieve my goals for the day. I have noted that I am very efficient in the early hours of the morning til around 1pm so I would set up my learning new theories/concepts in the morning and leave reflection to the afternoon.

In terms of learning alongside others I will be conscious of their routines and have an understanding that the best way that I learn is not necessarily theres. For facilitators and community representatives first and foremost interact with all with respect to them, of their time and their abilities.

When things are building up I have found taking some time away from the task to be wildly beneficial. Also to remember to break it down into bite sized actions and goals. If I get overwhelmed, which is inevitable, I will go for a break and write down a plan of how to approach the task from another perspective as the current path is obviously not working.
